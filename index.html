Readyness Camp
Readyness camp focuses on teaching ways to handle hard situations to teenagers. The minimum age for Readyness Camp is 15. Readyness camp is an internationally recognized camp, there is an application that needs to be filled out for entry. The camp intructors are ex military and S.W.A.T. The camp is cleaned everyday by a janitorial with compliance to the U.S. Health & Human Services regulations. Safety is the main priority at Readyness Camp, we implement very strict safety procedures at all of our different campus facilities.
